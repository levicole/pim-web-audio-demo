This is a demo of 1bit polyphony using Pulse Interleaving Modulation. This is a conversion of the demo provided by Blake Troise (aka PROTODOME) in his article "The 1-Bit Instrument: The Fundamentals of 1-Bit Synthesis, Their Implementational Implications, and Instrumental Possibilities"

The sound is a bit crackly, and I'm not sure if that's due to my implementation or JS not being able to keep up.
